Lag Analysis

Reading the chart: Same-day correlation (r = 0.445) is strongest but partly driven by shared seasonality. Best carryover lag = 1 day (r = 0.428), with a secondary cluster at 5–7 days — this is the adstock signal. Adstock values are model outputs, not inputs.

Month-of-Year Seasonality

Tax season (Jan–Apr) averages ~2.4× above rest-of-year baseline. Modeled explicitly so channel effects aren’t confounded by calendar demand.
