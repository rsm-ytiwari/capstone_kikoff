Suggested flow of presentation
Slide 1 - Project Cover Slide
Concise Project Name
Date of Presentation
Group Members
Slide 2 – Table of contents
Slide 3 - Group member summary
Brief introduction of group members with pictures
Slide 4 - Executive Summary
Headline: State the core business problem or analytical challenge.
Strategy: Highlight 2–3 key aspects of the approach, including any pivots (i.e. change in approach and why did you change).
Value that you have created so far: Summarize key insights, recommendations, or deliverables.
Slide 5 & 6 - Business Problem & Data Overview
Organization: Name, industry, what they do.
Problem: High-level description of the challenge and opportunity.
Constraints: Any limitations on data, timeline, or methodology.

Data Description: Type, structure, volume, time frame, key entities and known issues.
Slide 7, 8 and 9 - Deliverables and Solution Path
Deliverables: Describe desired outcomes (e.g., predictive models, segmentation, optimization).
Evaluation: Criteria for assessing usefulness of your solution.

Solution Path: Summary of key steps—EDA, segmentation, modeling, and evaluation techniques.
Slide 10 and 11 - Results and Findings
Data Review: Sources, completeness, structure, EDA summary.
Analytics Strategy: Modeling targets, feature engineering, preparation.
Analytics Results: Final models, performance metrics, limitations.
Recommendations: Key takeaways, business value, suggested actions.
Slide 12 Conclusion
Recap the journey from problem to solution.
Next steps and organizational impact.


Slide 13 Open Q&A
Slide 14 onwards - Appendix
